GRAYS

view project website

Grays is an advocacy of 128 non-binary genders that acquaints its viewers with the numerous relationships they can have with masculinity and femininity (both and neither), and the many different ways they can identify and express their gender regardless of the bodies they were born into.

Gender is a social construct—the means by which characteristics, interests, mannerisms and behaviours are deemed “masculine” or “feminine” relies on people creating, believing and reinforcing these divisions.

Our society isn’t binary: man or woman, male or female, masculine or feminine, no—real variation exists in every measurable attribute used to assign the categories of ‘male’ and ‘female’ to bodies. Facial hair, voice pitch, hormone levels, reproductive organs, external genitalia, as well as people who are intersex all prove that ‘biology’ is not a clear-cut basis for the gender binary.
