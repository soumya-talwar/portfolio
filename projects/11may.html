[ visit the whatsapp bot ]

The day before my birthday, I decided to program a Whatsapp bot to send me a compliment every hour throughout the day. Because nothing quite says "emotionally intelligent" like automating yourself some self-love.

ChatGPT is the most eloquent flatterer on the internet so obviously, I used the OpenAI API with a Twilio setup to build a bot that prompts ChatGPT every hour and delivers the generated compliment to my Whatsapp.

To make it meaningful, I spent some time reflecting on past wins & accomplishments, and paired them with a few photos. With those inputs, ChatGPT could generate compliments that were relevant, sincere, and only mildly suspiciously specific.

If you're curious, reach out to me or check out the code on my GitHub to build yourself your own hype bot.
